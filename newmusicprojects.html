MUSIC PROJECTS

While it may only be in the early stages, Double Dry Hop is a cover band playing music from the past five decades specifically in the rock, pop and alternative genres. The band is currently in the process of recording cover tracks, which are set to be released later this year. The band will soon be ramping up it's search for a seasoned, experienced drummer who has experience playing various music genres, and has an open mind in regards to choosing songs to cover. We plan on learning 30+ covers and are currently in the process of building our set list.

The goal of this cover band project is to not only play cover songs that an audience would recognize and be able to connect with, but we hope to also "think outside the box" a little and select songs that you will not only be familiar with, but songs that will also surprise you. Sorry, we will not be covering Don't Stop Believing by Journey and Living on a Prayer by Jon Bon Jovi. Don't get me wrong, I like both of these songs just as much as anyone but almost every cover band plays these songs. If you have songs suggestions please do not hesitate to contact us. We'd love to hear your ideas!
