BAND HISTORY

Hover over the images below to learn about previous bands. If your viewing this page on a mobile device, click on the images to view details.

Straw Wings

2013 - 2014

Straw Wings was a modern hard rock/metal band from Milton, MA. The band's song writing and overall sound was heavily influenced by RED, Evans Blue, Breaking Benjamin and Nickelback. When joining in 2013, the band was getting ready to release their first EP titled "Deface the Machine". The EP was released in the Fall of 2013 and is currently still available for purchase at CD Baby, Amazon, and Google Play.

Other Highlights: Straw Wings recorded a music video for the song Pieces, which can be seen on YouTube as well as on my Videos web page. The music video has close to 1,000 views. Straw Wings also had a strong social media presence on Facebook, Twitter and YouTube. You can also listen to tracks off of the EP at ReverbNation.com.

Modern Element

2011 - 2013

Modern Element was a hard rock/alternative band from Boston, MA. Formed by four seasoned musicians in 2011, the band started out playing only covers, before transitioning their focus on writing and performing originals.

Other Highlights: Gained exposure through open mics and performances at venues including The Lucky Dog, Copperfields and All Asia Cafe. Released a three-song EP and later released a full length album. Received recognition on NECN's The Morning Show Soundtrack segment, the online publication Warped Magazine, and also through Social Media sites including YouTube, Facebook and Twitter. Received radio airplay on local college and online radio stations.

Theoryshift

2009 - 2010

After the original lineup went their own ways in 2007, the vocalist reformed Theoryshift in 2009 with new members. During the new era, the band played a mixture of originals written by the previous and new band members. While keeping the traditional Theorysift sound and mixing in a heavier writing style, the band's short lived tenure was very memorable. Theoryshift was able to share the stage with well known local bands including Prospect Hill.

Other Highlights: Performed many gigs thoughout New England and even expanded their presence into NYC. Performances include Cask N' Flagon, The Lucky Dog, Uncle Mike's in NYC, and Artist Showcase Events hosted by Record Labels at Ralph's Diner and the Amsterdam Bar.

Inhale

2001 - 2007

Inhale was a hard rock/metal band from southeastern MA and was my most successful music project. The band spent most of it's time writing and playing originals, with mixing in an occasional cover song. The style of writing was influenced by many artists including Black Sabbath, Metallica and Tool.

Other Highlights: Played over 100 shows, including shows at The Paradise, Worcester Palladium, Bill's Bar, The Middle East, The Living Room and CBGB. Released two EPs and one full length album. Received radio airplay on college and commercal radio station including WAAF and Emerson College. Received positive CD Reviews in Metronome Magazine, The Noise and other local publications/magazines.

Syphon

2000 - 2001

Syphon was the first band I played in post high school. Together, we wrote over 10 songs and recorded a two-song EP at a local recording studio.

Other Highlights: Played gigs at The Sad Cafe in New Hampshire and also played a Talent Showcase at The Worcetser Palladium in 2001. We also played several parties which helped us gain more exposure, along with performing at several open mics. As mentioned above, we also recorded and released a two-song EP.
