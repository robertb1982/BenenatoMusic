MUSIC RESOURCES

Here are some general music industry resources that I recommend checking out.

CD Baby

Online music store specializing in the sale of CDs, vinyl and music downloads from independent musicians to consumers.

Sonicbids

Online platform that brings musicians together with people looking to book or license music.

BandMix

Online network for connecting local musicians and bands in eight countries.

The Deli Magazine

Independently owned website run by musicians and music writers, covering local music scenes.

Indie Minded

Boston (and UK)-based independent magazine that features the independent community.

Performer Magazine

Music magazine aimed at independent musicians. Provides musicians info on the music business.

BMI

Performing rights organization that collects license fees on behalf of songwriters, composers, and music publishers.

ASCAP

Membership association of more than 575,000 US composers, songwriters, lyricists and music publishers.

American Songwriter

Magazine that offers in-depth Q & Aâ€™s with up-and-coming, established and legendary songwriters and much more.

Harry Fox Agency

Provider of rights management and collector and distributor of mechanical license fees on behalf of music publishers.

Film Music Network

Provides leads for projects seeking music or composers, including film projects, television projects, corporate videos and more.

SESAC

Originally the Society of European Stage Authors and Composers, is a performance-rights organization.
