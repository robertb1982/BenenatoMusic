WELCOME!

Thanks for visiting! Being a musician for over 20 years and having a growing interest in web design, it makes sense that the first web site I have decided to build would focus on my music background. One element in my life that has always remained consistent has been my love for playing music. Check out some music in the Media section, and if you're a musician stopping by I've added some great web sites under Resources you may find helpful. Drummer Wanted For Cover Band

My new cover band project, Duble Dry Hop, is searching for a responsible and experienced Drummer to join their lineup. No drugs, No drama, No egos!!
